About Me

Ufuoma Avwenagha

Ufuoma Avwenagha is a tech enthusiast and self-taught creative artist. His love for art has led him on a continuous journey towards truth and self awareness. Outside art, he loves to take leisurely walks through the city while listening to music, cooking, watching anime and spending time with his family.

What he wants to awaken the most is people’s imagination with his art. He wants his art to be a mechanism that stirs imagination And soul.
